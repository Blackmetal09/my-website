The Core Idea

The "Within" project aimed to create an experiential game that delves into the complexities of internal conflict, particularly through the lens of addiction. Instead of a direct narrative, the game uses reactive environments and abstract mechanics to convey feelings of struggle, temptation, and the arduous path to self-awareness.

Reactive Mechanics

Player choices and in-game "states" directly influence the world. For example, succumbing to a negative impulse might cause the environment to become more distorted or oppressive, while moments of resistance could lead to clarity and more open spaces. This feedback loop is central to the player's empathetic journey.
